Privacy Policy

Warranty Tracker is an offline-first app. All data you enter (dates, notes, and the details you record) is stored only in local app storage on your device and is never transmitted to any server. The app collects no personal data, does not use analytics or tracking, and makes no network requests except the App Store's own purchase and subscription verification, which is handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit framework.

If you delete the app, your data is deleted with it. There is no account, no login, and no cloud sync.
